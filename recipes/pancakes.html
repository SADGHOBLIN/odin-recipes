Broccoli & Chicken Stir-Fry

A delicious chicken and broccoli recipe is a simple and effective meal that can be made with little effort!

This quick and easy meal can serve up to 4 people and takes only 30 minutes from start to finish.

Start with cooking the chicken in a pan, before adding any additional veggies and spices of your choosing.
Pop the chicken on a plate before proceeding to cook the broccoli.
Whilst the broccoli is cooking, get your sauce ready and start on the noodles!
Finally, add the chicken mixture back and cook until well-coated and sauce thickens.

Ingredients

  • Oil:
    2 teaspoons of canola oil.

  • Chicken:
    Approx. 450g of chicken thights, cut or diced into small pieces.

  • Onion:
    We reccomend 125g of a yellow onion, as to not be too sweet!

  • Ginger and Garlic:
    Just a pinch for some flavour.

  • Broccoli:
    Around 300g of fresh This recipe starts with a neutral oil, such as canola oil heated in a skillet.broccoli. Frozen also works, but fresh is always best.

  • Sauce:
    50ml of Soy sauce
    of stir fry noodles50ml of Oyster sauce

  • Noodles or Rice:
    Allow one portion per person, which is around 60g-100g

  • Seasoning:
    You can add whatever you like! We reccomend:
    1/4 teaspoon of crushed red pepper and
    1/4 teaspoon of black pepper.

Step by Step Process

  1. Step 1:
    Heat the oil in a large, non-stick pan over medium heat for about 2 minutes. Meanwhile, cut up the chicken thighs and prepare them to be cooked!

  2. Step 2:
    Add the chicken to the pan in a single layer and cook for aprroximately 6 minutes. Be sure to stir, and toss often, until the chicken is crisp and cooked through.

  3. Step 3:
    Whilst the chicken cooks, dice up your onions and add them to the chicken, along with the garlic and ginger. Stir often and watch for the onions to turn translucent. Once nice and fragrant, pop the contents of the pan on a plate.

  4. Step 4:
    Add the noodles or rice to a pot to boil for around 8 minutes, depending on your personal preference. If these are ready-to-wok, then wait until step 7. However, if you need to boil them first, then now is the time!

  5. Step 5:
    Put the broccoli florets into the pan, you don't need to boil. Cook for 2-3 minutes over a medium-high heat, stirring occasionally, until bright green and heated all the way through.

  6. Step 6:
    Meanwhile, whisk together the soy sauce, oyster sauce, crushed red pepper, and black pepper together in a small dish until smooth and even. This should take about 30 seconds.

  7. Step 7:
    Add the cooked chicken mixture back into the pan to cook with the broccoli. Turn the heat down to a low-medium, and in the sauce of stir fry mixture. Stir constantly until everything is well coated and the sauce thicken. You can either add your noodles or rice into the pan to mix with the sauce, or serve first with your chicken mixture on top.

  8. Step 8:
    Enjoy!
