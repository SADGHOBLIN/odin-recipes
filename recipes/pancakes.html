Banana Pancakes

Mouth-watering banana pancakes made from scratch will be sure to impress. Fun and easy, you are likely to already have most of the ingredients to hand!

This delightful meal can be made in as little as 10-15 minutes, and allow plenty of room to customise the recipe to include plenty of your favourite toppings.

Start with making the pancake mixture by combining the ingredients and stirring to make the batter.
Pour the batter into a lightly oiled pan in small portions to make the pancakes one by one! Be sure to have some fun and perfect your flip!
Serve your pancakes immediately with chopped bananas. You can be creative, adding your favourite ingredients: maple syrup, honey, peanut butter, whipped cream, strawberries, jam. Treat this as your blank canvas and craft your favourite combinations of fruits and sauces!

Ingredients

  • Flour:
    120g of all purpose flour.

  • Sugar:
    One tablespoon of white sugar.

  • Baking Powder:
    Two teaspoons of baking powder.

  • Salt:
    A little pinch of salt, 1/4 of a teaspoon.

  • Eggs:
    One egg, beaten.

  • Milk:
    200ml milk of your choosing.

  • Vegetable Oil:
    Two tablespoons of vegetable oil.

  • Bananas:
    Two ripe bananas, either chopped or mashed.

  • Other:
    Add any other ingredients of your choosing! Whether it is maple syrup or strawberries, just have fun with it.

Step by Step Process

  1. Step 1:
    Start with measuring out all of your ingredients and getting them ready.

  2. Step 2:
    Combine the dry ingredients into one bowl: the flour, white sugar, baking powder, and salt.

  3. Step 3:
    Stir both mixtures together into a large bowl to create the pancake mixture. The batter will be slightly lumpy - this is normal!

  4. Step 4:
    Next, heat a frying pan with a touch of oil on medium heat. Pour small-medium sized portions of the batter into a pan. These are your pancakes, so make them as big or as small as you wish.

  5. Step 5:
    Cook the pancakes one by one until they are golden brown on each side. It should take approximately 3-5 minutes. This is the time to show off your pancake flipping skills!

  6. Step 6:
    Serve your pancakes hot! Top with anything that you like, but we reccomend drizzling over some maple syrup, with some chopped fruit! Yum!
